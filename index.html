🔒 Your Privacy Comes First

We are committed to keeping your data safe and private. Here is what we promise:

✅ We do not sell your personal data — ever.

✅ We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party marketing platforms.

✅ Location is optional — it is only requested when you open the Nearby map feature, and only with your explicit permission. You can use the entire app without enabling location.

✅ Ghost Mode — when Ghost Mode is active, your location is invisible to other users and is not shared with our servers.

✅ You are in control — you can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

When you create an account, we may ask you to provide:

  • First name and display name
  • Phone number (for secure OTP-based login)
  • Profile photos (uploaded voluntarily by you)
  • Age, bio, and interests (for profile creation)

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when you use the Service. It may include your device's IP address, device type, operating system, and session information. This data is used only for security monitoring and improving the Service.

Location Data (Optional – Nearby Feature Only)

Location access is entirely optional. We only request your device location when you open the Nearby tab within the app. We never request location in the background, before login, or without your knowledge.

  • Location is used only to show you and other users on the Nearby map.
  • When Ghost Mode is enabled, your location is hidden from other users and is not sent to our servers.
  • You can enable or disable location access at any time via your device settings.

We do not use location data for advertising or profiling.

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including matching features, messaging, and the Nearby map.

  • To manage your account and give you access to the features available to registered users.

  • To send notifications — such as new match alerts and message notifications — only with your permission.

  • To improve the Service — we analyse anonymised usage patterns to enhance app features and performance.

  • To ensure safety — we monitor for abuse, fraud, and guideline violations to keep the community safe.

  • To respond to your requests — when you contact our support team.

How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data only in these limited situations:

  • With infrastructure providers: We use trusted cloud providers (such as Google Cloud) to store and process data securely. These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements.
  • With other users: Your public profile information (name, photos, interests, bio) is visible to other WhatsLeft users as part of the matching experience.
  • For legal compliance: We may disclose data if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests from authorities.
  • With your explicit consent: For any other purpose, only if you have given us clear permission.

Retention of Your Personal Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service. Specific retention periods:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, and for up to 30 days after account deletion (to handle any outstanding requests or disputes), after which it is permanently deleted.
  • Location data: Not stored persistently on our servers. Only the most recent position is held temporarily, and only while Ghost Mode is off.
  • Usage logs: Up to 12 months for security and service improvement purposes.

Transfer of Your Personal Data

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. Where required by law, we ensure that adequate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses, to protect your data during international transfers.

Delete Your Personal Data

You have the right to delete your personal data at any time. You can delete your account directly from the app settings. Upon deletion, your profile, photos, and messages will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

You may also contact us to request access to, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you.
