## Full Description (≤4000 chars)
Discover meaningful connections with WhatsLeft, the modern dating app designed to help you find someone who truly gets you.

**Meet People Near You**
WhatsLeft shows you real profiles of people in your area. Browse at your own pace, and when you feel a connection — swipe right. When it's mutual, it's a match!

**Smart Swipe Cards**
Our beautifully designed swipe cards make discovery feel effortless. Swipe right to like, or pass on profiles that don't feel right. No pressure, just smooth and intuitive interactions.

**Real-Time Chat**
Once you match, start chatting instantly. Our real-time messaging keeps your conversations flowing — no delays, no disappearing messages.

**Nearby Map (Optional)**
Curious who's around you? The Nearby tab shows you people on an interactive map. Your location is only ever used when you open this feature — never in the background, and never without your permission.

**Ghost Mode — Your Privacy, Your Choice**
You decide when you're visible on the map. Go Ghost anytime to browse without being seen. Full control is always in your hands.

**Safe & Verified**
We take safety seriously. All accounts are verified via phone number, and you can block or report anyone at any time.

**Premium Features**
Upgrade to unlock unlimited likes, see who liked you, and more.

WhatsLeft is free to download. Premium features are available via in-app purchase.

## Permissions Explanation (for Play Store Review)

| Permission | Reason |
|---|---|
| CAMERA | So you can take profile photos directly from the app |
| READ_MEDIA_IMAGES | So you can choose photos from your gallery for your profile |
| ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION | Only used when you open the Nearby tab to show people near you. Never used in background. |
| ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION | Coarse backup for location on older devices, same contextual use as above |
| INTERNET | Required for all core app functionality — profiles, chat, and authentication |
| BILLING | For optional in-app premium subscription purchase |

**Note for reviewers**: Location permission is requested **only** when the user navigates to the Nearby tab. It is never requested during login, onboarding, or any other screen. Users who decline location permission can still use all other features of the app normally.
